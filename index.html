Distribution of Food and Essential Items to the less priviledged children

As part of our mission to reduce hunger, Nlekota Charity Foundation carried out a food and essential items distributon exercise aimed at supporting deprived children in the community. The outreach was designed to address immediate hunger and provide basic support to children from vulnerable households. During the program, over 900 children were fed and recieved essential items to support their well-being. The activity brought relief and joy to many families, especially those facing economic hardship,and helped ensure that the children had access to nutritious meals for the day. Nlekota Charity Foundtion remains committed to reaching more communities and continuing initiatives that promote health, dignity, and the well-being of every child.

School Health Program: Promoting Hygiene and Handwashing

As part of our mission to preserve life, Nlekota Charity Foundation organized a school health outreach aimed at improving hygiene practices among children. During the session, pupils were taught the importance of personal hygiene, proper handwashing techniques, and how these simple habits can prevent common diseases. The program was interactive and engaging, with demonstartions on correct handwashing steps and discussions on keeping their environment clean. At the end of the session, edutaional materials were distributed to support their learning and encourage regular school attendance. The outreach was part of our ongoing commitment to promoting health, education, and well-being of children..

Immunization Outreach in Duste Community

A part of our mission to preserve life, Nlekota Charity Foundation partnered with a Primary Health Care Center to promote routine immunization among children. The outreach focused on educating parents and caregivers about the importance of vaccines in protecting children from preventable diseases such as measles,polio and tuberculosis. In addition to the education sessions, eligible children recieved their routine immunizations at the health center. This collaboration highlights the power of partnerships in imporving community health. Nlekota Charity Foundation remains committed to supporting immunization efforts and ensuring that every child has the oppourtunity to grow up healthy and protected
